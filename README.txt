In this repository you find 5 data sets used in the SNSF Spark project CRSK-2_220746 "Machine Learning Modular Forms".

Data_1 contains data of 238 modular forms. The data consists of:
- The level of each form
- The weight of each form
- A label "Eis", "new" or "old" indicating if the corresponding form is an Eisenstein series, a new cuspidal form or and old cuspidal form, respectively.
- The first 101 Fourier coefficients of each form, starting with the 0-th coefficient.

Data_2 contains data of 163 new cuspidal forms consisting of:
- The level of each form
- The weight of each form
- The first 168 Fourier coefficients of each form of prime index, starting with the prime 2.

Data_3 contains data of 1195 new cuspidal forms consisting of:
- The level of each form
- The weight of each form
- The first 168 Fourier coefficients of each form of prime index, starting with the prime 2.

Data_4 contains data of 1027 new cuspidal forms consisting of:
- The level of each form
- The weight of each form
- The first 168 Fourier coefficients of each form of prime index, starting with the prime 2.

Data_5 contains data of 1330 new cuspidal forms consisting of:
- The level of each form
- The weight of each form
- The first 200 Fourier coefficients of each form, starting with the 1st coefficient.
